Meet Bryan

Bryan M. Truyol, Esq. is the founding partner of Truyol Legal, a Miami-based divorce and family law firm. He is a bilingual attorney who is passionate about helping clients navigate the often complex landscape of family law.

Mr. Truyol has a bachelor’s degree in English and earned his Juris Doctorate degree from Florida International University College of Law, having graduated in the top 10% of his class. During that time, he served as the founding president of the Family Law Society, earned the highest grade in the class in several courses, was awarded the Legal-Medical Partnership Clinic Award and the Pro Bono Service Award, interned for the Domestic Crimes Unit at the Miami Dade County State Attorney’s Office, and interned for the Honorable Marcia Caballero in the Eleventh Judicial Circuit of Florida. Additionally, Mr. Truyol has previously worked at other private law firms.

Born and raised in Miami, Mr. Truyol believes in giving back to his community. He often serves as a guest speaker at local schools, including his alma mater, Christopher Columbus High School. He has also been involved in the Miami-Dade Put Something Back Program representing clients on a pro bono basis as well as serving as an Adjunct Professor for the FIU College of Law Domestic Violence Project and Community Lawyering Legal Clinic, in which he supervised law students in the areas of domestic violence and family law.

Recently, Mr. Truyol was published in Barry University School of Law’s Child & Family Law Journal and was featured as a guest commentator on Telemundo’s Un Nuevo Día.
